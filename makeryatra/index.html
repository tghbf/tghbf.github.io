About This Events

“India lives in its villages” - Mahatma Gandhi.


MakerYatra is an enlightening journey to explore, connect and learn from the rural India.
The first MakerYatra, one of its kind, is happening in Idar, a town surrounded by rocky mountains of Aravali range in North Gujarat.
You will explore amazing talents in agriculture, robotics, aviation, engineering and education which will be a life-changing experience.
Let us get together to feel what talent is lying unidentified by the world by being a connecting link to take them for scaling up their insight for a larger good by making a connection with the right people at the right time.
You can be a part of the MakerYatra with a vision of making a change for the sustainable goals for developing India.
We are looking for participants from diverse background to have people from all walks of life to get insights into the growing rural community thinking for the betterment of rural India.
Let’s make a path unitedly for nation-building.

FAQs

Any Question?

Who can participate in the MakerYatra?
Anyone aged over 17 years with an open mind who wants to explore, get inspired and contribute to Rural India can participate.
What are my duties/expectations as MakerYatri?
Comply with the facilities given to me.
Execute to the duties given to me in time.
Be punctual so that schedule is maintained.
Be proactive, so collectively we can to meet the objectives of MakerYatra.
What are the accommodation facilities provided?
Accommodation will be provided on sharing basis in common rooms etc. in Gram Panchayats, Schools, Hostels, and Dharamshalas with limited facilities.
What are the food facilities during the MakerYatra?
Basic meals, common to all, will be given as per the local availability and facilities. Morning Tea-Breakfast, Lunch, Afternoon Tea, Dinner.
What are the expenditure for the MakerYatra?
You will have to come on your own expenditure to reach Idar. Overall cost of food, travel and accommodation for 4 days sum up to approximate Rs 2500/-. However deserving applicant can also be provided full scholarship on case to case basis. Check Scholarship tab in FAQ for more details.
What is the selection procedure the MakerYatra?
Yatris will be selected on the basis of their application form. The 3rd section of the application form is the most important. Selected Yatris will receive an email informing them about their selection with payment options. Once payment is done Yatri will be confirmed.
What kind of varied exposures/experiences MakerYatra will offer?
Agriculture, Seed Research, Drip Irrigation, Organic farming, Village management, Mechanical Fabrication, Robotics, Pesticide & Fertilizer, Dairy, Animal Husbandry, Health Problems, Co-Operative Initiatives, Community Farming, Nursery, Vegetable growing, Fruit Farming, Cold Storage, NGO Visit, etc. It will be limited to the time, venue and person availability. Meeting face to face with the amazing rural role models. Identifying Pain Points of this area.
