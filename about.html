About Us

Working towards sustainable ecosystem in rural India.

We have been working in this direction since 10 years with different interventions. We have been encouraged by the outcome and outputs at the student level and community level.

We are fortunate to be instrumental to inculcate maker spirit in the children from rural and tribal areas of north gujarat with the help of competent collaborators.

  • Provide urban-like quality education to the rural sector
  • Aspire to bring the digital advantages to Rural area
  • Engage Youth in development sector to achive SDGs

At TGHBF we are always trying to scale the impact by sharing por way of working with other partner NGOs
